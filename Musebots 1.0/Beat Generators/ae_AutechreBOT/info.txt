## version ##

06.19.15

## instrumentation ##

A bot that generates beats with odd subdivisions.

type:
	@beats
	@drums
	@samplebased
	@processed

## platform ##

Max standalone, built from Max 6.1.8 on Macintosh.

## behaviour ##

creates its own form. Lots of (potentially extreme) signal processing.

## messages ##

sends: 

	@hdensity - relative activity (# of onsets per measure).
	@subdivision - current subdivision

receives:

	@exiting - will exit if told to do so by another musebot.
