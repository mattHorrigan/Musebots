## name ##

blank_BOT

## version ##

1.0

## instrumentation ##

plays a simple triangle wave synth

type:
     @template
     @synth

## platform ##

Max standalone, built from Max 7.3.1 on Macintosh.

## behaviour ##

demonstrates receiving and sending messages, and configuring a musebot.

## messages ##

sends:
     @hdensity - probability of playing a note

receives: 
     @notepool - pool of MIDI pitches from which it can select
