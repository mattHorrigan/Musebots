## name ##

ae_TextureBOT

## version ##

06.01.15

## instrumentation ##

Generates granular textures based upon the current notepool

type:
    @texture
    @granular

## platform ##

Max standalone, built from Max 6.1.8 on Macintosh.

## behaviour ##

Requires a huge directory of analyzed files (SampleBank)

## messages ##

sends:
    @vdensity (percentile of active voices)

receives:
    @notepool - (0.-1.)
    @hdensity - (0.-1.)
