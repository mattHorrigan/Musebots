## name ##

ae_AtmosphereBOT

## version ##

05.20.16

## instrumentation ##

type:
    @texture
    @soundscape
    @resonant

## platform ##

Max standalone, built from Max 6.1.8 on Macintosh.

## behaviour ##

Generates atmospheric texture by resonating soundscape recordings

## messages ##

sends:
    @nothing

receives:
    @notepool (will play lowest note);
    @exiting (will exit when it receives exiting message).
